Our facilities

Our unmatched infrastructure functions as a ladder to our student's success and makes them future-ready.

World Class Library

Multiple libraries stocking lakhs of books and e-books catering to different colleges inside the campus. The Central Library is the soul University. It holds a hybrid collection of printed as well electronic resources, which include books, journals, e-journals, reports, case studies, training manuals pertaining to all subjects under various courses.

Largest Playground

Our College has an excellent play ground for students with indoor and out door sports facilities. Our college is having very big playground for various outdoor games. Various sports are conducted by coachers.

Tasty and Healthy Food

The University has also set up two eateries, which provide delicious muffins, sandwiches, different flavours of coffee, soft drinks, and piping hot snacks. The joints have comfortable seating arrangements and provide respite to the students after their gruelling academic schedule.
